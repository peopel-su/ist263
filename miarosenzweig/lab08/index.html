Mia Rosenzweig's Biography

At age 3, Mia discovered her passion for playing the best sport ever, soccer. She played soccer almost every day of her life until she was 18. She's played on multiple club and school teams. Additionally, she has participated in numerous tournaments all over the country. Her love ultimately led her to play other sports, including lacrosse, track, tennis, golf, and ski.

Mia's first job was working at a summer camp in Westchester NY called Future Stars. Here she was able to share her love for soccer with a younger audience. She made new friends and learned a lot about herself.

Currently, Mia is studying Economics and Data Analytics at Syracuse University. She often thrives in male-dominated spaces. Additionally, she participates in the consulting club and University Girl Magazine. She is exploring her passion for business and technology.

Personal Information
